Privacy Policy / 隐私政策

Effective Date: May 27, 2026

English Version

We value your privacy. Screen Share CastNow ("the App") utilizes direct End-to-End Peer-to-Peer (P2P) connections for casting and mirroring. Please read below for our detailed privacy commitments:

1. Information Collection

We do not collect, store, or process any personal data, account credentials, or media streams. Specifically:

  • Media Stream Data: Your video and audio transmission flows directly between the broadcasting device and receiving browser/device. No stream content ever touches our servers.
  • No Logs: No screen activity, keystrokes, or transmission logs are stored.

2. Information Sharing

Since we do not collect any personal information, we do not share, sell, or trade any user data with third parties.

3. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, you may contact us at: mingh.liu@gmail.com.
