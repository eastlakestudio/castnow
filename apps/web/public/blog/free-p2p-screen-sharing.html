2. What is Peer-to-Peer (P2P) Screen Sharing?

  • The Direct Tunnel: Explain that P2P creates a direct pipeline between the presenter's computer and the client's screen, skipping central streaming servers entirely.
  • The Privacy Advantage: Because data isn't processed on a third-party server, sensitive product IP is vastly safer from interception or logging.

Since bandwidth isn't capped by a central server bridging 50 users, a 1-to-1 P2P connection can utilize maximum available bitrate. This means crisp 4K text rendering and smooth 60fps animations.

B. Zero Installation (WebRTC)

The magic of modern browsers. Your client just clicks a link (or inputs a 6-digit code) and immediately sees your demonstration. Period.

C. Low Latency

Near-instant cursor movement and typing speed, ensuring that when you say "look at this chart," they actually see it right away.

D. It's Free

Without the need for expensive relay servers running 24/7, P2P tools like CastNow can offer robust features completely for free.
